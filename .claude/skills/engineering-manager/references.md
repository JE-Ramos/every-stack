## RTFM — Essential Reading

### Books
- **The Manager's Path** — Camille Fournier. The definitive guide to engineering management, from tech lead to CTO. Practical, opinionated, and grounded in real experience.
- **An Elegant Puzzle: Systems of Engineering Management** — Will Larson. Systems thinking applied to engineering organizations. Covers sizing teams, managing technical debt organizationally, and succession planning.
- **High Output Management** — Andy Grove. The foundational text on management as a leverage function. Concepts like "managerial leverage" and "task-relevant maturity" are timeless.
- **Turn the Ship Around!** — L. David Marquet. A masterclass in leader-leader (not leader-follower) management. The "intent-based leadership" model is directly applicable to engineering teams.
- **Radical Candor** — Kim Scott. Framework for giving feedback that is both caring and direct. The 2x2 of "care personally" and "challenge directly" is a daily tool.
- **Team Topologies** — Matthew Skelton & Manuel Pais. How to organize engineering teams for fast flow. Essential for any manager making team structure decisions.
- **Accelerate** — Nicole Forsgren, Jez Humble, Gene Kim. The research behind DORA metrics and high-performing engineering organizations. Data-driven management.

### Docs & Articles
- **Rands in Repose** (https://randsinrepose.com/) — Michael Lopp's blog on engineering management. Decades of practical wisdom, especially on 1:1s and organizational politics.
- **Lara Hogan's Blog** (https://larahogan.me/) — Resources on management, sponsorship, and giving feedback. Her "Manager's README" concept is widely adopted.
- **re:Work by Google** (https://rework.withgoogle.com/) — Google's people operations research, including Project Aristotle (psychological safety) and structured interviewing guides.
- **The Engineering Manager's Booklist** — A curated list maintained by the engineering management community.
- **Will Larson's Blog** (https://lethain.com/) — Deep dives on engineering strategy, organizational design, and the staff+ IC track.

### Courses & Talks
- **Engineering Management Fundamentals** — Various conference talks by Camille Fournier, available on YouTube.
- **Crucial Conversations Training** — VitalSmarts. Essential skill-building for difficult conversations.
- **Manager Tools Podcast** — Mark Horstman. The most comprehensive free resource on management fundamentals (1:1s, feedback, delegation, coaching).

### OSS Projects to Study
- **Engineering Ladders** (https://github.com/jorgef/engineeringladders) — Open-source engineering career ladder framework.
- **Progression Framework** (https://github.com/Monzo/progression-framework) — Monzo's open-sourced engineering progression framework.
- **Manager README Templates** — Various open-source templates for manager self-documentation.
