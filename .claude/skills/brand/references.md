## RTFM — Essential Reading

### Books
- **Building a StoryBrand** — Donald Miller. The clearest framework for brand messaging: your customer is the hero, your company is the guide. Eliminates the #1 brand mistake (making it about you, not them)
- **Positioning: The Battle for Your Mind** — Al Ries & Jack Trout. The original positioning framework. Written in 1981, still the most cited brand strategy book. Understand how categories work in the human mind
- **Start with Why** — Simon Sinek. Why purpose-driven brands outperform feature-driven ones. The Golden Circle framework (Why → How → What) is foundational
- **Perennial Seller** — Ryan Holiday. How to create work that lasts — relevant for building brands that endure beyond trends. Focuses on the timeless over the viral
- **The Brand Gap** — Marty Neumeier. Short, visual, and powerful. Bridges the gap between business strategy and creative execution. Introduces the "brand as a person's gut feeling" definition
- **Obviously Awesome** — April Dunford. Primarily a positioning book, but positioning is the foundation of brand. Read this alongside The Brand Gap
- **Contagious: Why Things Catch On** — Jonah Berger. The science of virality: STEPPS framework (Social Currency, Triggers, Emotion, Public, Practical Value, Stories). Essential for understanding why some brands spread

### Articles & Frameworks
- [Every Business Is a Meme — Garry Tan](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/Jl-garry-s-channel-6-skills-for-successful-startup-founders) — Garry Tan's framework on why brand and storytelling are founder-level skills
- [Positioning Canvas — April Dunford](https://www.aprildunford.com/post/what-is-positioning) — Practical worksheet for defining your brand's competitive context, value, and differentiation
- [The Arc of Brand Narrative — Andy Raskin](https://andyraskin.com/the-greatest-sales-deck-ive-ever-seen-4e06e3397e5a) — How to structure your brand's narrative arc (used by Zuora, Salesforce, and others)
- [How to Build a Brand That People Love — First Round Review](https://review.firstround.com/) — Collection of brand-building case studies from First Round portfolio companies
- [Eugene Wei's "Status as a Service"](https://www.eugenewei.com/blog/2019/2/19/status-as-a-service) — Foundational essay on how social platforms and brand intersect with status dynamics

### Courses & Resources
- [Seth Godin's altMBA](https://altmba.com/) — Intensive workshop on marketing, storytelling, and leadership — taught by the godfather of modern marketing
- [The Futur — Chris Do](https://thefutur.com/) — Business of design and brand strategy education for creative professionals
- [Brand New (Under Consideration)](https://www.underconsideration.com/brandnew/) — Archive of brand identity reviews — invaluable for developing visual taste and vocabulary

### Tools & OSS
- [Figma Community](https://www.figma.com/community) — Free brand guidelines templates, design system starters, and presentation templates
- [Coolors](https://coolors.co/) — Color palette generation and exploration tool
- [Brandmark](https://brandmark.io/) — AI-powered logo and brand identity generation for rapid prototyping
